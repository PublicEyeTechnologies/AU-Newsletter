Athletic Union Newsletter
Today sees the return of the Athletic Union Newsletter. Following a survey of all AU Members in January a recurring request was for more information on the Athletic Union and greater profile for respective teams, so this is exactly what we have done. Every other Tuesday we will focus on two different sports, highlight something to look out for in the coming weeks and give you a brief update on all that has been happening within the Athletic Union.
If you have any content you would like us to include, please contact Jessica Burke-Martin.

AU Exec Catch-Up

On the 10th February, the stage was set, the lights were dimmed, and the AU were out in full force. This night, was the return of AU Takes Over Hey Ewe Final. After an impressive 95 press ups from Cricket, a huge 6 minute plank from Waterpolo, and an incredible fight from all 10 finalists, the wrath of Gymnastics was all too much for the other clubs involved. Cricket came in at an impressive second, and Waterpolo, third.
A massive thankyou to all teams involved, and to our AU Exec Events Co-ordinators, Josephine, Alex and Sarah, and to all our volunteers for making the night possible.
