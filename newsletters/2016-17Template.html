VP Sport Catch-Up
The last couple of weeks have been another busy period, with changes to the AU Exec, Loughborough Sport Reps in Halls for the 2016-17, the AU Alumni Weekend and of course AU Ball just round the corner.
The area to highlight this week is the introduction of Welfare Reps for the next year. These Reps will be a go to for AU Club Members who need someone to talk to and will be able to signpost students to areas that cab offer them support in all areas of their life. They will also run initiatives and help promote AU campaigns. If this sounds interesting please apply here to our Welfare training session on Sunday 15th May.

One to Watch
We have some important information for you about the AU Ball, please keep an eye of Facebook or click here to find out more...
This week saw another huge victory for Loughborough Students Tennis after an incredible showing at their world varsity over the Easter Break. The men's first team won over Bournemouth which resulted in a promotion to the BUCS national Super league.
After a mixed start, ending 1-1 after the doubles, Loughborough stormed through the singles, taking it 4-2 overall.
The first bank holiday weekend, can only mean one thing, Loughborough University will welcome back its former students in a number of festivities across the University Campus. The Athletic Union was among those welcoming back those who had led our continuous record of 35 years of BUCS champions.
