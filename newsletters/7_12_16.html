Athletic Union Newsletter
Welcome to the AU! This newsletter will tell you what the AU have been up to recently and allow you to find out what is coming up soon. If you have any queries, or would like to register interest to be a part of the next newsletter, please email aumedia@lsu.co.uk

Sport Executive Officer Update - Luke Thomson
The last couple of weeks have been highly eventful. First, we interviewed and subsequently appointed a new Spectatorship Officer on the AU Exec, in the form of Norm Sadiq. We then hosted a number of club presentations as they applied for AU Grant Funding. All interviewed applications were of a high quality, so well done to the successful clubs. Following this, we ran two Big Matches for Basketball and the Kick-boxing fight night, both of which were attended by over 200 spectators who were treated to two high class and dramatic events.
The updated AU constitution, passed by Club Chairs, was formally approved by the Union’s Board of Trustees and can now be found on the newly created AU wiki (auwiki.lsu.co.uk). We also announced an AU partnership with performance analysis giants Hudl, launched the second addition of this years WoW magazine and started selling our charity Naked Calendars, and progressed with plans for the AU’s upcoming Mental Health campaign TeamTalk
In the last Newsletter of this term, the final thing for me to say is after a busy first term, with the re-launch of WoW, a host of fabulous social and sporting events, and of course some top quality sport. On behalf of the Athletic Union, we hope you have an enjoyable holiday and wish you a very Merry Christmas.
Yours in Sport,

AU Exec Update - Norm Sadiq: Spectatorship Officer
Hi, I'm Norm and I'm your new spectatorship officer! Last year I was the AU Exec Alumni officer. I hope to Encourage a Sporting Culture across campus, among both students and the local community. Following the successes at the Rugby Union BIG Match and more recently the Men’s Basketball BIG Match, we would like to ensure spectatorship becomes a vital part of the Loughborough Experience. The sub-committee along with Viv have done a great job; and working together with the other sections of the AU and LSU Media, we hope to continue showcasing the high calibre athletes who proudly wear African Violet and everything for the University. In addition to regular fixtures on campus each week, we have two huge events coming up next term: Following last week’s difficult trip up north, we are looking forward to righting some wrongs when we “welcome” Durham to home turf on the 01/02/2017 for D-DAY.
I hope you all have a great Christmas break and look forward to showcasing the great atmosphere and support the Loughborough crowd can bring.

Dance Competition and Winter Showcase: On Saturday 3rd of December, Loughborough Students’ Dance Club’s External Competition Squad travelled to York University Dance Competition to compete against 9 other universities. This was our first competition of the year and was hugely successful with the team coming home with first place in their category! This is a great achievement as the team had only 8 sessions in order to prepare for the competition as well as being a great start to the year for what looks to be a very successful one for the club! We will also be competing at two other competitions later in the year at Manchester and Northampton, as well as at home in March.
This week the club also has their very first Winter Showcase on Thursday the 8th in Martin Hall Theatre at 7pm! All proceeds of this event will be going to charity and entry is £2. There will be 15 performances across the evening, as well as cakes and refreshments. Please do come along and support for an evening of fun, food and dancing!
