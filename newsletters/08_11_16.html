Sport Executive Officer Update - Luke Thomson
With freshers week done and dusted and normal Uni life restored, the AU is getting into full flow. Last week we launched our first big match with over 300 students coming down to support. Furthermore, our first term of the committee training programme will be completed by Thursday. Going forward, I am currently working on a grant funding application process for clubs; planning a huge scale Loughborough Sport Awards and will have to present my strategic year plan to the Union Board of Trustees shortly.
AU Exec Update - Holly O'Shea; Welfare Officer
I'm Holly O'Shea the AU Exec Welfare Officer. With the role being new this year a lot of my focus has been establishing how welfare fits into the AU and what approach we want to take. We've decided to focus on mental health this year, beginning with our campaign Team Talk to be launched towards the end of this term! The campaign focuses on encouraging AU members to open up about their issues and look after one another. We're hoping to do this by providing clubs with mental health in sport training and holding awareness events to make mental health a more commonly spoke about topic within our AU.

Loughborough Ski Trip On Friday the 16th of December, leaving from either Loughborough, London or Dover, we will be heading out to the crazy, mad land of Pas de la Casa. This place has 210km of skiing, where you can access the whole of the Grandvalira ski area for no upgrade cost! Situated over 2000m above sea level and with snow already out there, your skiing will last all day long. However if you dont want to partake in any night skiing, Pas is also known as "Ibiza on Ice". Being a tax free haven, people swarm to experience the cheapest ski resort around. We love to share the best week of the year with everyone, so feel free to bring all your friends from home. Booking closes 18th of November though so get booking ASAP!
